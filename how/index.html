How it Works

Inspired by 4-track tape recorders — equipment used by great musicians of all training and genres over many years to create countless masterpieces — Exquisite Corpse is designed for people who are both interested in and intimidated by making music.

The platform is a means of arranging different voices, both near and far, as each sings a piece of the same song – with the end product being an Exquisite Corpse-style piece of karaoke that users can download, save, share, and re-visit amongst friends.
